Seed 0:0 Mirror World

Discovered: Unknown

Origin File: looking_at_you.dat

Mirror World

“0:0” is not a normal seed. You cannot enter it manually — the game generates it on its own. Most players report that the light comes from the wrong direction, mobs have no shadows, and the player model moves with a one-frame delay behind actual input.

Documented Anomalies

  • Light Source: The sun is visible but casts no shadows. The light does not bounce from the ground.
  • Shadow-Creatures: Dark silhouettes appear in tree shadows but disappear when approached.
  • Model Desync: Pressing F5 rapidly causes the model to stare at the player for a moment before correcting.
  • Village Rooms: Under every village, a hidden room contains a single sign: “You weren’t supposed to see this.”
  • Portal: Instead of the Nether, the portal sends the player to a dark mirrored version of the world.

Lore & Theories

Some believe this seed is a leftover testing environment. Others claim it connects corrupted worlds drifting in the game’s unused memory space. The fact that the Nether leads to a “dark reflection” of the overworld suggests that the seed generates a mirrored dimension rather than a separate world.

Evidence

One of the captured scenes from the Mirror World. The silhouette on the other side performs the player’s movement two seconds earlier.

Recovered Files

The seed folder was found partially corrupted. Most files were unreadable, except for one: LOOKING_AT_YOU.dat. This file appears to be the seed data for the Mirror World.

Attempts to open other files result in errors or display garbled data. It is speculated that the corruption occurred during an unexpected shutdown while the world was active.
