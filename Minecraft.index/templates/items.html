Items

An item is an object that can exist within inventories of players, mobs, or armor stands; in various storage blocks (like chests or droppers), as well as in item frames and glow item frames.

Thrown items

  • If an item that does not become a block is dropped, it becomes an entity represented by a sprite that floats above the ground. It remains for 5 minutes in a loaded chunk before despawning (except for the nether star), unless the player walks over it to pick it up, it is picked up by a mob, hopper or minecart with hopper, or it is destroyed by fire, lava, cactus, explosion, void, or /kill command.
  • Items can be thrown by the player using the drop key (default: Q) or by using /drop command.

Stacking

Most items can stack to 64. This includes all full blocks, whose outlines take up an entire block when placed. Some items, such as snowballs, empty buckets, eggs, signs, and ender pearls, can stack up to a maximum of 16. Some other items do not stack at all, such as anything with a durability (weapons, armor, and tools), filled buckets, and potions. All items can technically be forced to stack up to 99‌[JE only] or 127‌[BE only] using commands.
