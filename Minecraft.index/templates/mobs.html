Release: June 2010

Creator: Markus Persson "Notch"

Mob

In Minecraft, mobs are living entities that can move around and interact with the world. They can be passive, neutral, or hostile, each with unique behaviors and characteristics.

Mobs Types

  • Passive Mobs: These mobs do not attack the player and often provide resources or companionship, such as cows, sheep, and villagers.
  • Neutral Mobs: These mobs will only attack if provoked, like wolves and endermen.
  • Hostile Mobs: These mobs actively attack players and include zombies, skeletons, and creepers.
  • Boss Mobs: Powerful mobs that present significant challenges, such as the Ender Dragon and the Wither.
